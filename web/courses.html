Courses

International workshop (40 hours)
"Free & low cost sport tools for training and research"

We come to your workplace: University, Club, Medical center.
We teach on how to use our tools as well as other free tools like Kinovea, Longomatch and R to measure in sports.
We encourage students to use their laptops to measure and evaluate.
Students will receive a certified document from Chronojump.
