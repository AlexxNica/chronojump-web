Fixing photocells

Recomendations on fixing the photocells to a tripod or bar.

A.- Reflector in tripod

  1. Remove the mounting screw from the top of the tripod.
  2. Remove the cover of the mounting screw.
  3. Attach the mounting screw with the reflector.
  4. Mounting screw attached with the reflector.
  5. Reflector fixed in tripod. Position A (only available on some tripods).
  6. Reflector fixed in tripod. Position B (all tripods).

B.- Photocell in tripod

  1. Attach the mounting screw to the photocell case.
  2. Photocell fixed in tripod. Position A.
  3. Photocell fixed in tripod. Position B.

C.- Photocell or reflector in a bar with velcro

  1. Fix velcro to a bar using double sided tape.
  2. Put velcro on the photocell.
  3. Attach photocell to bar.
  4. Attach reflector to bar (first put velcro in reflector).
